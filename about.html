About Green Apple Cafe

The Green Apple Café is owned by Cindy Lorentz Robinson, a lifelong North Andover resident and graduate of both the French Culinary Institute in New York and Merrimack College. After ten years as a chef at the Crane Estate, she opened Green Apple Café to bring fresh, locally sourced food to her community.

We proudly serve breakfast and lunch crafted from local ingredients, featuring gluten-free options and Fair Trade coffee. Whether you’re dining in or grabbing takeout, every dish is made with love and care.

The Green Apple Café also offers catering for events of all sizes — from small gatherings to large celebrations. Let us bring our kitchen to your table.

At The Green Apple Café, we believe that great food and a welcoming atmosphere go hand in hand. Every dish we serve is crafted with care, using fresh, locally sourced ingredients that reflect the flavors of our community. From our signature brunch plates to our hand-blended teas, every detail is made to comfort and delight.

We’re more than just a place to grab a bite — we’re a gathering spot for laughter, connection, and celebration. Whether you’re joining us for one of our cozy afternoon tea parties, a seasonal tasting event, or a special celebration with friends, we strive to make every visit feel memorable. Step inside, take a seat, and let us treat you to a warm experience that feels like home.

Join us soon — there’s always a cup of tea and a friendly hello waiting for you!

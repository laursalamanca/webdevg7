Afternoon High Tea

Afternoon High Tea is always a delightful occasion at The Green Apple Café! Enjoy freshly baked scones with Devonshire cream or lemon curd, assorted finger sandwiches, hearty soup, sweet treats, handcrafted chocolates, and premium loose-leaf tea served in elegant china. Reservations are required.

Create your own charming tea outing or reserve a spot at one of our seasonal Saturday tea parties at noon! We also now offer a more intimate tea service perfect for smaller gatherings of up to eight guests — or even just you and a friend.
